Bed & Breakfast

We pride ourselves on our cosy, comfortable bedrooms. We have three double bedrooms and one twin available for our guests.

The rooms look out on to delightful views of farm and woodland and the peace is only broken by birdsong and the bubbling river Marlais .

Breakfast is either continental (juices, cereals, croissants) or full traditional (locally produced bacon & egg, lamb's kidney, mushrooms, tomatoes, toast & marmalade) served with tea or fresh coffee.

If required a 4-course evening meal can be provided for £20.00 plus VAT.

During the day, visit some of the local attractions - castles, Aberglasney gardens, the National Botanic gardens of Wales, Dinefwr Park - or trek on the Black mountain. Alternatively, stay more local and visit the ancient church at Llansadwrn or explore the farm and woodlands. Cycling and, on a wet day, swimming are available locally.

Llandeilo (6 miles) offers a wide range of attractions and a rich history. More about Llandeilo.
