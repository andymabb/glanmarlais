Welcome

If you're looking somewhere to stay in the heart of rural mid-Wales, Glanmarlais is just the place.

From a delightful Georgian farmhouse on a 103 acre organic farm, you can explore the beautiful Towy valley on the outskirts of the Brecon Beacons National Park, visit the local castles and gardens or roam throughout our farm and woodlands.

Alternatively, stay in the farmhouse and catch up on all the books you have been meaning to read, listen to music and browse through our own extensive library for some literary inspiration.

We will provide you with a continental or full traditional breakfast for the first meal of the day.

About Us

Glanmarlais is an organic farm in the beautiful Towy valley in mid-Wales offering comfortable bed and breakfast accommodation. Nestled deep in the Welsh countryside, the farm has a rare cattle breed (Irish Moyled) as well as native Welsh Black sheep.

Records for the estate go back to the Armada year of 1588. The present buildings date from the eighteenth and nineteenth century and are of traditional stone and slate construction.

Of Glanmarlais' 103 acres, nearly 30 acres are broadleaf and conifer woodlands with pastureland accounting for the rest and the river Marlais running through the lower pastures. Glanmarlais in Welsh means the banks of the Marlais.
